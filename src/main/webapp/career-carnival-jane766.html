FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You’re Thinking, Answered Here

Who can participate in the Career Carnival?

Working professionals, freshers, and final-year graduates with relevant technical skills can participate.

Is this a guaranteed job offer program?

No. Candidates are evaluated through a structured process, and offer letters are issued only by companies based on performance and hiring needs.

Do I need prior work experience to take part?

No. Freshers and final-year students can participate if they meet the skill requirements for the roles.

How are candidates evaluated during the Career Carnival?

Evaluation is based on performance in an online hackathon, company-aligned assessments, and an AI-based technical interview.

Will I be interviewed by actual hiring companies?

Yes. Companies review evaluated candidates and issue offer letters directly based on their selection criteria.
